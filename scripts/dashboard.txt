## 2Ô∏è‚É£ General Business Dashboard (Dashboard.pbix)

Project Objective:
To create an interactive business performance dashboard showing revenue, profit, and operational metrics for decision-making.

Data Preparation:

 Performed data cleaning in Power Query
 Built relationships between fact and dimension tables
 Created Date table for time intelligence
 Defined calculated measures using DAX

Key KPIs:

 Total Revenue
 Total Profit
 Total Orders
 Profit Margin %
 Growth %

Visuals Used:

 KPI cards
 Clustered bar charts
 Trend line charts (monthly/yearly)
 Matrix tables with drill-down
 Slicers for Date, Category, Region

DAX Measures Examples:

 Total Sales = SUM(Sales[Amount])
 Profit Margin = DIVIDE([Profit],[Sales])
 YoY Growth measures

Insights Delivered:

 Monthly and yearly performance trends
 Category-wise contribution
 Region-wise profitability
 Growth tracking over time
